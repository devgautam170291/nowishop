Return Policy

Our Returns Policy is a plan implemented by Nowishop specifically regarding the terms of trade, replacement and/or refund offers. All items in different categories have slightly different return policies. For all items, the policy on the item page will explain the general returns policy. Please refer to the respective item's applicable return policy on the product page for any exceptions to the table below.
The return policy is separated into three sections; please peruse all segments to understand the conditions and cases under which the returns will be accepted.

Part 2 - Returns Pick-Up and Processing
In case of returns where you would like item(s) to be picked up from a different address, the address can only be changed if pick-up service is available at the new address
During pick-up, your product will be checked for the following conditions/issues:
Category Conditions
Correct Product IMEI/ name/ image/ brand/ serial number/ article number/ bar code should match and MRP tag should be undetached and clearly visible.
Complete Product All in-the-box accessories (like remote controls, starter kits, instruction manuals, chargers etc.), freebies and combos (if any) should be present.
Unused Product The product should be clean, unused, unwashed, unsoiled, without any stains and with non-tampered quality check seals/ warranty seals still intact (wherever applicable).
Undamaged Product The product (including charging port, back-panel etc. if any) should be undamaged and without any scratches, dents, tears or holes.
Undamaged Packaging Product's original packaging/ box should be undamaged.
The field executive has the right to refuse the return if any of the above conditions are not fulfilled.
For any items for which a refund is to be given, the refund will be handled once the returned item has been received by Nowishop.
Section 3 - General standards for a successful return
  1. In certain instances where Nowishop can't process a replacement for any reason, a refund or different offer might be given to the customer on a case by case basis. (Terms and conditions apply)
  2. In the case of receiving a faulty item during delivery, you will be given a refund or the most ideal offer we can give you. Once you have acknowledged a delivery, no returns will be processed, aside from manufacturing defects. In such cases, the replacement/return general conditions will be applicable.
  3. For furniture, any item related issues will be checked by an authorized staff member (free of cost) and will be dealt with by replacing the defective/faulty piece of the item. Full replacement will be given only in situations where the authorized staff acknowledges that replacing the defective/faulty part won't resolve the issue.
