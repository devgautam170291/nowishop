We realize the importance of strong fraud detection and having a system for resolving these issues. Nowishop and our online payment partners are consistently monitoring transactions for suspicious activity in order to flag potentially fraudulent transactions for manual verification. What is a 3D Secure password?
The 3D Secure password is a system enforced by VISA and MasterCard in partnership with card issuing banks under the "Verified by VISA" and "Mastercard Secure Code" services. This system is an additional layer of security that utilizes identity verification for online credit/debit card transactions. It has the user create a password that is only known by the user to ensure no fraudulent activities occur.
